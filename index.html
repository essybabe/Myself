ESTHER ORJI-AMAKONZE

ABOUT MYSELF

When you have big dreams about how to serve other people,
it can really be exciting. But when you have dreams about
how to advance yourself, it can be frustrating because you start
dreaming small. And I want young people to know that once you
have brains, you have flexibility.

-Esther Orji-Amakonze

Esther Orji-Amakonze was educated at Caritas University and with a Bsc. in Accounting from Enugu state, Nigeria.
